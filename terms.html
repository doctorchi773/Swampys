TERMS

VCHIX is a modern American restaurant located in the heart of Chicago. We offer a unique dining experience that combines the best of both worlds: classic American cuisine with a modern twist. Our menu features a variety of dishes, all made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. We also have a wide selection of wines and cocktails to choose from.

Our restaurant is designed to be a gathering place for friends and family. We have a casual atmosphere with a warm and inviting vibe. Our staff is friendly and attentive, and we are always happy to help you choose the perfect dish and beverage.

We believe that food should be enjoyed, and we are committed to providing our guests with a memorable dining experience. We hope you will join us soon for a taste of VCHIX!

Our Philosophy
We believe that food should be fresh, flavorful, and made with care. That's why we source our ingredients from local farmers and purveyors whenever possible. We also believe that food should be fun, and we take a creative approach to our menu. We are always looking for new and innovative ways to use fresh ingredients.

We also believe that dining should be an experience, and we strive to create a warm and inviting atmosphere at VCHIX. Our restaurant is designed to be a gathering place for friends and family. We want you to feel comfortable and relaxed, and we want you to enjoy your time with us.

Our Menu
Our menu features a variety of dishes, all made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. We have something for everyone, from classic American fare to more contemporary dishes. Here are a few of our most popular items:

The VCHIX Burger: Our signature burger is made with 100% Angus beef and topped with American cheese, lettuce, tomato, and onions. It is served on a toasted bun and comes with your choice of fries or a side salad.

Our Wine List
We have a wide selection of wines to choose from, including both red and white wines. We also have a selection of sparkling wines and dessert wines. Our wine list is constantly changing, so be sure to ask our sommelier for recommendations.

Our Cocktails
We have a creative cocktail menu that features both classic and modern cocktails. We also have a selection of beer and spirits. Our bartenders are happy to create a custom cocktail for you, so be sure to ask them what they recommend.
